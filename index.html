Eid al-Adha Mubarak

12th Dhul Hijjah 1447 AH

May Allah accept our worship

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Significance of Eid al-Adha

Eid al-Adha commemorates Prophet Ibrahim's willingness to sacrifice his son Ismail as an act of obedience to Allah.

It is a time of joy, family gatherings, and sharing with others.

Eid Prayers

May your Eid prayers be accepted and your day be blessed.

Dua for Eid

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِي عِيدِنَا وَفِي دِينِنَا وَفِي دُنْيَاَنَا

O Allah, bless us in our Eid, our religion, and our worldly life.

May this Eid bring peace, happiness, and blessings to all Muslims around the world.
